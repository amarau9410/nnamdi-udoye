About Me

I've spent the last 15 years in the trenches of enterprise IT, which taught me one thing: technology is only as good as the friction it removes. Over the past 3+ years, I've pivoted that mindset fully into the Salesforce ecosystem, focusing on building systems that do not just work, but actually scale.

Right now, I am the Lead Service Cloud Admin for a global fintech firm, where I support a massive user base and over 150,000 international merchants. One of my proudest wins was designing advanced Screen Flows to automate contact-to-account linking, transforming a manual process into a 100% accurate data stream.

What I do day-to-day:

  • Solve for scale: I use Screen, Record-Triggered, and Scheduled Flows to remove manual bottlenecks.
  • Tighten governance: I led migration from leaky Profiles to a scalable Permission Set model for stronger audit readiness.
  • Enterprise DevOps: I manage CI/CD with Copado and Azure DevOps to keep releases predictable and downtime at zero.
  • Protect data integrity: I focus on root-cause analysis and large-scale remediation to keep reporting accurate.

Before fintech, I spent nearly a decade in the student accommodation sector, managing a national estate of 150+ properties. That is where I strengthened my expertise in Marketing Cloud and Field Service Lightning, bridging digital campaigns and operational delivery.

I am a 4x certified Salesforce professional who values the human side of tech. Whether I am briefing executives or collaborating with vendors, my goal is always the same: keep the platform stable, the data accurate, and the users happy.
